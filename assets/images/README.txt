TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
================================================================
- Always compress images before uploading (use squoosh.app or tinypng.com)
- Keep hero images under 300 KB after compression
- Always include descriptive alt text — see HTML files for the pattern used
- Use real job photos whenever possible — stock photos reduce trust
- For the service area map: export from Google Maps > Share > Embed, screenshot at 2x and scale down
- Reviewer photos: only use with explicit written permission from customers
