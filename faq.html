Pricing & Estimates

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is typically due upon job completion — we don't ask for full payment upfront, and a reputable contractor never should. For larger projects we may collect a small deposit at scheduling, which we'll discuss clearly during the estimate.

Financing availability varies — ask us about current options when you call. For urgent safety work that needs to happen regardless of timing, we'll always do our best to find a solution that works for your situation. We'd rather help you get a dangerous tree down safely than have it sitting there because of budget timing.

Tree work is priced based on the size and species of the tree, its location relative to structures, the complexity of the removal or trim, access for equipment, and what's included (stump grinding, debris removal, etc.). There's no one-size number for any job, which is exactly why a site visit matters. We give written, itemized quotes so you know exactly what you're paying for.

We can give you a rough ballpark over the phone if you describe the tree and situation, but we never give binding quotes without seeing the job in person. Access, lean direction, nearby hazards, and soil conditions all affect pricing and can't be assessed from a description alone. Our in-person estimates are free, and the quote you get will be accurate.
